Airtable CRM Backend

Managing customer relationships across 20+ brands requires powerful, scalable tools. Airtable gives us everything we need—for free. We can build email lists, run targeted promotions, track customer engagement, and collect valuable data without relying on expensive third-party services.

The Power of Data: Every brand becomes an active touchpoint. When customers show interest in specific content, we respond immediately with targeted campaigns. No brand sits dormant—they all work together to drive engagement and sales.

With our proven blueprint from scaling Manna Hydration, we now know exactly what it takes to grow a brand. Airtable CRM helps us decide which brands to scale next by showing us what customers actually want. Features like customer segmentation and automated campaigns—previously only available to our high-tier Shopify stores—are now accessible for every site.

Already integrated with our QR code system, Airtable transforms how we connect with customers. Instead of websites collecting dust online, they become intelligent touchpoints that capture interest, build community, and generate revenue.

Core Render Portal

Managing 3D render requests across multiple brands and products requires a centralized system. The Core Render Portal is our custom-built project management platform designed specifically for visualizing products before they're manufactured.

Why It Matters: When launching new products across 20+ brands, we need to see them first. 3D renders help us visualize designs, test color schemes, and make decisions before committing to production—saving time and money.

Built with a robust tech stack, the portal offers:

  • Team Collaboration: Email invitations, user profiles, and team-based organization
  • Project Management: Create, edit, and track render requests with due dates
  • Dynamic Theming: Each team gets custom colors and branding
  • Progress Tracking: Visual dashboards for project status and timelines
  • Email System: Automated notifications for project updates and invitations
  • Project Library: Centralized repository of all renders with searchable metadata

The Core Render Portal bridges the gap between concept and reality. Whether it's a new Manna Hydration bottle design or a BKLYN Steel espresso machine variant, every product gets visualized, reviewed, and perfected in the portal before production begins.

Brand Ecosystem App

What if customers never needed to leave our brands? Just like TikTok keeps users scrolling by combining content and commerce, we can create immersive brand ecosystems where shopping and content consumption happen in one seamless experience.

The Vision: Build our own content hub—our own YouTube, our own social platform—all integrated with Shopify. Customers browse products while watching brand content. No friction between discovery and purchase.

Imagine a Manna Hydration member who opens our app and immediately sees:

  • Latest hydration tips and workout content
  • New product launches with instant purchase options
  • Community challenges and rewards
  • Personalized recommendations based on their activity
  • All without leaving the ecosystem

This isn't just about selling products—it's about building loyalty through ecosystem membership. When customers identify as part of the Manna community or the BKLYN Steel family, they're not just buying bottles or espresso machines. They're buying into a lifestyle, a movement, a world we've created for them.

The technology exists. The skills are in-house. Now it's about execution—creating these worlds for each brand and watching them come alive.

In-Person Events & Activations

Digital connection is powerful, but face-to-face engagement creates brand advocates. Pop-ups, community events, and activations put our brands directly in front of people, building real relationships that translate to lasting loyalty.

Events We're Planning: From run clubs to pickleball tournaments, DJ pop-ups to food court collaborations—every activation is an opportunity to turn strangers into community members.

For Manna Hydration and BKLYN Steel, events become part of the brand identity:

  • Run Clubs: Weekly group runs sponsored by Manna—hydration that powers your performance
  • Coffee Workshops: BKLYN Steel hosts espresso masterclasses, teaching craft and artistry
  • Pickleball Tournaments: Active lifestyle events with product sampling and giveaways
  • DJ Pop-Ups: Music, culture, and brand lifestyle collide in memorable experiences
  • Food Court Collaborations: Partner with local food vendors, create shared experiences
  • Company Partnerships: Team up with complementary brands for co-marketing

These aren't just marketing tactics—they're community building. Every event gives us PR opportunities, user-generated content, direct customer feedback, and real human connection. People remember experiences. They remember feeling part of something bigger than a product.

We're already doing this well. The goal now is to scale the playbook—document what works, refine the approach, and roll it out across all brands that benefit from community presence.
